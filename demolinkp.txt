>>>This project showcases HealthAI, an intelligent healthcare assistant built using Gradio and Hugging Face Transformers.
Features include:

🤖 AI Doctor Chat (LLM-powered)

🔍 Symptom-based Disease Prediction (Rule-Based)

💊 Automated Treatment Plan Generator
The entire app runs in Google Colab and uses lightweight open-source models like google/flan-t5-base for efficient performance.
